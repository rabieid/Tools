Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

Our tool supports JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. You can compress these formats or convert between them.

Is there a file size limit?

There's no strict limit, but very large images (over 10MB) may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities.

How does image compression work?

Compression reduces file size by removing unnecessary metadata and optimizing how image data is stored, with minimal impact on visual quality.

Are my images stored or sent to a server?

No, all processing happens in your browser. Your images never leave your device.

What's the best compression level to use?

We recommend starting with 80% quality. Adjust higher for critical images or lower for faster loading times.
